Oh no! Some Sableye got trapped in some crystals while searching for more gems. Try and free them all! Make sure to Left Click onto the canvas so it can capture your mouse pointer and let you control the camera with your mouse! Click Escape to uncapture the mouse.

Controls

Right Click to spawn blocks in front of you (right click the same spot to start building a stack)

Left Click lets you break blocks you've made (top to bottom if the blocks are in a stack) as well as break the crystals the Sableye are trapped in! Note: Admittedly this can feel a little jank. If you find yourself not breaking the block you want to break, try moving a little back or a little forward! Same goes with adding blocks.
